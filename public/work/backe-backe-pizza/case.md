    body: >
      The campaign centers on an augmented reality music video triggered
      by scanning the Gustavo Gusto packaging: a tomato character checks
      the tech before the three Deine Freunde band members appear on top
      of the pizza. From there, kids can scratch the pizza to spread
      cheese and tomato or switch into a karaoke mode and sing along —
      turning the box itself into a playable music video. The campaign
      extended across video ads, animations, in-store displays, the pizza
      packaging itself, posters, and social content.
