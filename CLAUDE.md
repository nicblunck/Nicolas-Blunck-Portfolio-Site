# Working with Nicolas

Nicolas is a designer, not a backend/software engineer. Avoid unexplained technical jargon (frameworks, parsers, APIs, build tooling, CI/CD, deployments, etc.) — when a technical term is necessary, briefly say what it means in plain language. Favor concrete, visual explanations (e.g. "this file" / "this folder" / "this is what shows on the page") over abstract architecture talk. When reporting on PR/CI activity, translate status into what it means for him in practice (e.g. "the site rebuilt fine and you can preview it here" rather than just "CI passed").
